# In the Bay Area, we want to play folk music seriously.

The story behind this is actually quite simple.  
Recently, I went to see Lao Lang perform.  
Before the show was even over, I was pulled back through several years of memories.

And suddenly I realized—  
The Bay Area has no shortage of great music or great bands,  
But opportunities to sit down and listen to an evening of "folk music" seem to be few and far between.

So we thought:  
Why not put on a show ourselves?


## This isn't a "nostalgia-only" performance

We'll play some classics you might hum along to,  
And also sneak in a few original songs,  
Because—folk music isn't a museum, nor a tape recorder.

It's not just about "youth, distant places, summers we can never return to,"  
But about life in this moment, emotions unfolding right now,  
And the reason you're sitting here tonight.


## This is a concert among friends

On stage, you'll find a group of people who love folk music,  
And also friends who have long been involved with music.

There's no pressure of a "lineup announcement,"  
No "who's the star" setup,  
Just a group of people, taking the music seriously.


If you:

- Have ever been struck by a song on the road  
- Believe folk music shouldn't only live in memories  
- Want to hear a different kind of live show in the Bay Area

Then come.  
Bring friends, or just bring yourself.

We'll take care of getting the music ready,  
You just need to come and help us complete this performance.
